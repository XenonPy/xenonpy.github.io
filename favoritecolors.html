Design Palettes

Monotone

This colorless palette, great for use with monotone photographs, is professonal and artistic. However, you should combine it with a bright accent color specific to your design to highlight links.

Blu Ocean

This palette, used on the Bluspace website, is a great pallete to use when you need to display images of unknown palettes, such as user posts. It looks good with the blue color taking up both most of the page or only a little.

Flame

A palette of fire like this one is difficult to incorporate with some images, but the results are spectacular if executed well. The accesability on this pallete is variable depending on how each of the colors are used.
